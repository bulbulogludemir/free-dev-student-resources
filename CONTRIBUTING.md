# Contributing

When adding or changing an offer:

1. Prefer the vendor's official page or official help center.
2. Add `last_checked` date if you refresh the whole dataset.
3. Mark uncertain offers as `check_current_campaign`, `school_dependent`, or `active_discounted` instead of forcing them into `active`.
4. Do not add account resale, fake verification, borrowed access, or terms-of-service bypass methods.
5. Keep claim steps short and practical.
